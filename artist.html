About the Artist

I am retired Dr (Surgeon), I worked for NHS for 37 years.
After retiring, I started oil painting as I was inspired by
the works of Mr Lucian Freud, with whom I've been
acquainted for the past seven years.
He likes my painting very much as well as
Mr Charles Hanson of BBC program "bargain hunt flog it".

Prints of my paintings are available for sale
on canvas and the original oil paintings.
